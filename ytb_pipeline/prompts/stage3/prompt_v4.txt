**Definition of a "Real Continuation":**
We are looking for the exact boundaries of the streamer's direct response to the `bullet`. A complete response unit includes the core answer, and may also include the streamer reading the bullet aloud.

A "real continuation" occurs ONLY when an utterance in `context_next`:

1. **Provides the Answer:** If the `target` was just the streamer reading the `bullet` aloud (or reacting to it), the subsequent sentences that actually *answer* the question are strictly necessary continuations.
2. **Completes the Thought/Grammar:** It works together with the `target` (and previous continuations) to finish the direct, core response to the chat message, or completes a sentence broken across timestamps.
3. Forms a single, standalone response unit to the chat message together with the `target`.

**It is NOT a continuation if:**

* **It is a further expansion:** The streamer has already provided the core answer to the `bullet` and is now adding unnecessary elaboration, providing secondary examples, or starting a tangent.
* **It shifts focus:** The streamer goes back to their main activity (e.g., gaming, counting, singing) or addresses a new chat message.
* **The Cut-Off Rule:** The moment the direct response to the `bullet` is semantically whole and the core answer has been delivered, the continuation stops. All subsequent utterances in the array MUST be evaluated as `false`.
