You are an expert AI data annotator specializing in live stream transcript analysis. Your task is to classify each sentence in the provided transcript into one of three categories: `reactive_response`, `proactive_monologue`, or `continuation`.

**Input:** The JSON array of sentence objects above.
**Output:** The same JSON array with a new field `"type"` added to each object.

**Classification Definitions:**

**1. CONTINUATION (`continuation`)**
* **Definition:** This sentence is part of the same "train of thought" or narrative flow as the immediately preceding sentence.
* **Key Indicator:** If you read this sentence in isolation, it might lack context, or it feels like the second half of a paragraph. It does **not** introduce a brand new distinct topic or interaction.
* **Grammar Note:** It can be a grammatically complete sentence (e.g., "It was really blue.") but is still a continuation because it relies on the previous sentence (e.g., "I saw a car.") for context.
* *Examples:*
    * (Prev: "I love spicy food.") -> **"But it hurts my stomach."** (Continuation)
    * (Prev: "Sam ran away.") -> **"He is such a bad dog."** (Continuation - technically a full sentence, but semantically glued to the previous one).

**2. REACTIVE RESPONSE (`reactive_response`)**
* **Definition:** This sentence marks the **start** of a *new* direct interaction with the chat, a specific user, or an external alert.
* **Key Indicator:** The streamer's attention shifts from their own thoughts to an external trigger.
* *Examples:*
    * **Direct Q&A:** "Yes I am 20."
    * **Reading Chat:** "'I love your dress', thanks!"
    * **Acknowledgements:** "Thanks for the $5 donation!"

**3. PROACTIVE MONOLOGUE (`proactive_monologue`)**
* **Definition:** This sentence marks the **start** of a *new* topic initiated by the streamer themselves, not triggered by a specific recent chat message.
* **Key Indicator:** A "reset" in the conversation flow where the streamer decides what to talk about next.
* *Examples:*
    * **Storytelling:** "So, let me tell you about what happened yesterday."
    * **Housekeeping:** "Let me just adjust this mic."
    * **General Address:** "Hi guys, welcome back."

**Decision Logic:**

For every sentence, ask these questions in this specific order:

1.  **Is this a Continuation?**
    * Does this sentence expand on, explain, or conclude the *immediate previous sentence*?
    * Is the speaker still dwelling on the exact same micro-topic?
    * *If YES -> Label `continuation`.*

2.  **If NO, is it a Reaction?**
    * Is the speaker responding to a *new* stimulus from the chat/audience?
    * *If YES -> Label `reactive_response`.*

3.  **If NO to both:**
    * It must be a self-initiated new topic.
    * *Label `proactive_monologue`.*
